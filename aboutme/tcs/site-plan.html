    About Us

    TCS building. Texas Capitol Semiconductor, Inc. (“tcs”) was founded in 1993 in Austin, Texas and has now been servicing the semiconductor industry for over 25 years. tcs services and repairs a broad range of Turbomolecular (“Turbo”) and Cryogenic (“Cryo”) Pumps. Since 1993, tcs has remained at the forefront of high vacuum technology by specializing in the complexities of magnetic levitation technology and controller circuitry and software. With our combined experience and expertise, tcs continues to provide premier vacuum pump repair solutions to top-tier semiconductor companies throughout North America and Europe. Please contact us to learn more about our capabilities and what we have to offer.

    Display of company image. The value of the official website was clarified at the beginning of planning, perhaps the organization did not expect it to play more marketing roles. But when introducing your own brand, you usually tell the other party that you can go to the official website to find relevant information. At this time, the official website is a business card of the brand on the Internet; The channel through which visitors identify with the brand through the official website. Pay attention to the brand image, although the website is small, but the details will be very textured, and strive to make visitors recognize the brand through the official website.

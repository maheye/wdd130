About Us

Texas Capitol Semiconductor, Inc. (“tcs”) was founded in 1993 in Austin, Texas and has now been servicing the semiconductor industry for over 25 years. tcs services and repairs a broad range of Turbomolecular (“Turbo”) and Cryogenic (“Cryo”) Pumps. Since 1993, tcs has remained at the forefront of high vacuum technology by specializing in the complexities of magnetic levitation technology and controller circuitry and software. With our combined experience and expertise, tcs continues to provide premier vacuum pump repair solutions to top-tier semiconductor companies throughout North America and Europe. Please contact us to learn more about our capabilities and what we have to offer.

We thank you for choosing and trusting our service.
